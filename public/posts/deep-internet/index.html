In this course, we've tried to better understand how we work together to build stuff.

The "stuff" has been a "MacGuffin".

"MacGuffin" is a term coined by Alfred Hitchcock to mean a 'thing' that motivates the characters in a movie, that could be a different thing, without changing the movie.

The internet is not what it used to be, and it's not what it will be.

I fully expect that most, if not all, of you will have careers that will span several iterations of the internet, several instances of it being one thing, and then being something else.

I do strongly encourage you to get to know the history, and the weird corners of the internet.
